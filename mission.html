Boxing and Life

Life is a seriouse of experiences. Those experiences often call-up certain emotions like joy, sadness, excitement, and fear. Those emotions serve as anchors for our memories. Those anchors can automatically assign 'meaning' to the challenges we learned from, if left unchecked.

The self-talk becomes "How did this event make me feel?" as opposed to "What did I learn from this, so I can duplicate or avoid this feeling in the future?"

If the initial emotion trigerred by the event was negative and if that feeling was not reframed into a learning experience - then that example of self-talk becomes dangerous.

So, reframing is a criticall skillset. Emotions are only one part of what makes us human.

Another part is our mind, our ability to think. This human quality gives us tremendous power to control our lives.

Life is not easy, but it can be simple.

The simplicity comes from embracing this sentence: In Life, There is Only Winning and Learning.

Failure is a myth. Failure is only a result of a negative emotion added to an experience.

Our human ability to think, change course, and grow holds the key to life.

We can decide at EVERY moment to define our life according to what we say. And our WILL is what will see us through.

It is 100% up to you to create your meaning and shape your lifestyle.
