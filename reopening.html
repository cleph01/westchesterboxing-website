COVID-19
Reopening Protocols

Sanitary conditions have always been a top priority at Westchester Boxing Club. The safest workout environment is extremely important to us - for your safety and for ours as well. In keeping with this goal, we are implementing the following protocols until further notice:

Cleaning Protocols

We are adding a rigorous, deep-cleaning and disinfecting process after each training session. Yes, we will disinfect the gym after every session. Our whole staff of trainers and team members will work together to support these cleaning protocols.

Health Checks

Members and our teams will have body temperatures taken prior to entering the club via touchless thermometers. Per the CDC guidelines, individuals with a body temperature greater than 100.4 degrees will not be permitted entry into the gym.

Revised Gym Capacity Scheduling

Members will continue booking in advance. Access to the workout area will be restricted to one member - one trainer per session. Workouts will be limited to 60 minutes to allow for a 30 minute deep clean immediately after. Bookings will be on a first come first serve basis.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Per building management, everyone is required to wear face masks upon entering the building. Anyone without a face mask will be denied entry. Our teams will be provided with face masks and gloves, which will be required to be worn in the gym at all times.

Notifications

Members will be asked to alert Nick at 914.329.6287 immediately if they test positive for COVID-19 within 14 days of their last club visit. We will contact all potentially exposed persons if there is a confirmed case of COVID-19 at the gym.
