Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a class?

Every class is 45 minutes long, from start to finish. The class is broken down into three segments. Each class begins with a 10 minute warm-up, followed by eight alternating rounds of bag work stressing technique and form of boxing fundamentals, with a specific focus on strength, conditioning, and cardio exercises. The class then ends with a 5 minute cool down.

What should I bring/wear to my first class?

All class require you to have your own personal set of gloves and hand wraps. If you do not have any, no need to worry, we have different brand gloves and wraps you can purchase at our Club. We also suggest wearing gym apparel and proper sneakers. Also, we suggest bringing water to drink during your workout since you will be breaking a sweat. No need to worry if you forgot your water, we have water for purchase!

Do I need to have previous boxing experience to take a class?

No need to worry if you have no prior boxing experience. We offer training to tailor your boxing experience whether it is your first boxing session or even if you have prior boxing knowledge and experience.

Are there age restrictions?

For our personal training you must be 6 years of age or older and for our classes you must be 18 years of age or older.

How many people are typically in a Fit Body Boxing class vs a Fit to the Core class?

In our Fit Body Boxing class we allow up to 10 members at a time only. In our Fit to Core class we only allow 4 members at a time.

What are your Club monthly membership fee?

At our Club we have no monthly enrollment fees, no annual fees, no membership fees, and no cancellation fees.

Do I need to ‘get in shape’ before I take a class?

No, our workout are tailor made to suit each individual member’s needs and skill level.

How can I get started?

You can call and schedule your appointment today!

Am I going to get hit?

At our club we are a no contact Club.

How is Westchester Boxing Club different from other boxing/kickboxing Clubs?

Westchester Boxing Club is different than other boxing/kicking Clubs in the area because we are the oldest boxing club in Westchester with experienced amateur and boxing trainers.

I don’t have gloves, can I borrow a pair from you?

All members are required to have their own gloves and wraps for hygienic purposes. Gloves and wraps are available for purchase at the WBC store.
