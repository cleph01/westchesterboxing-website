Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a session?

Every personal training session is 60 minutes long, from start to finish. The session is broken down into three segments:

  1. Warm-up and stretch.
  2. Alternating rounds of technique and form work. Depending on level of experience, focus will be on boxing fundamentals, agility, conditioning, strength and cardio exercises.
  3. Cool down.

What should I bring/wear to my first session?

All training sessions require you to have your own personal set of gloves and hand wraps. If you do not have any, we have different brands of gloves and wraps you can purchase at our Club. We also suggest wearing gym apparel and proper sneakers. Also, don't forget to bring water to drink during your workout since you will be breaking a sweat. No need to worry if you forgot your water, we have water for purchase!

Do I need to have previous boxing experience to sign up?

No need to worry if you have no prior boxing experience. Our training is tailored to your boxing experience. Whether it's your first boxing session or even if you have prior boxing knowledge and experience.

Are there age restrictions?

You must be 6 years of age or older.

How many people will be in the Gym?

Access to the workout area will be restricted to one member - one trainer per session until the CDC and other authorities provide further guidance.

What are your Club monthly membership fee?

At our Club we have no monthly enrollment fees, no annual fees, no membership fees, and no cancellation fees.

Do I need to ‘get in shape’ before I come my first session?

No, our workouts are tailor-made to suit each individual member’s needs and skill level.

How can I get started?

Click here to call (914) 329-6287 and schedule your appointment today!

Am I going to get hit?

No. Our White Plains club is 'No Sparring'.

Sparring is available through our program at W.C.C.

How is Westchester Boxing Club different from other boxing/kickboxing Clubs?

We are the oldest boxing club in Westchester County with experienced amateur and boxing trainers.

I don’t have gloves, can I borrow a pair from you?

All members are required to own their gloves and wraps for hygienic reasons. Gloves and wraps are available for purchase at the WBC store.
