WBC VS THE COMPETITION

The information below is full of reasons why We Are The Best Independently Owned And Longest Running Boxing gym in Westchester, NY.

We are NOT a franchise.

Why is this important?

Franchises have large monthly fees they owe to the frachisor, which comes from your monthly dues.

Since we are not a franchise:

  • We can bring you a fair pricing structure.
  • You are able to speak to the owner directly if you have any questions or concerns.
  • Nick and staff are always ready to meet your needs.
  • There are NO fake sales pitches that lock you into a contract with a ton of fees.
  • You only pay for your actual training sessions where you get the undivided attention of each and every trainer you're paired with.
  • NO overcrowded classes.
  • NO sharing of gloves.
  • NO undisclosed fees.

UFC Gym/LA Boxing

  • UFC Gym/LA Boxing has an Initiation Fee, but they do not disclose the COST of their initiation fee.
  • You are required to pay Recurring Dues and Taxes in advance!
  • UFC charges the following: Enrollment Fee, Processing Fee, Monthly Dues.
  • First and last month dues paid up front.
  • You are locked into an open ended contract.
  • They also charge you a Termination Fee, even if you're just changing your residency! It is $100 if half of your membership contract has not been completed, $50 if you have completed half of your membership.
  • They also charge you $15 if you lose your membership card! After all of the fees and membership you have already paid for, would you want to be charged for an accident? They also charge the same amount for a Late Fee.
  • UFC Gym will also charge you $20 if you don't show up to a session or even cancel an hour before!

Info can be found in their “Membership Policies” section.

​Westchester Fight Club

  • They do not disclose exactly what you're getting in your 30 day free-trial.

What is there to hide?

* It's VERY IMPORTANT to know what you are getting yourself into before you agree to sign up for any membership! Here at Westchester Boxing Club we DO NOT hide anything from you! *

  • They trap you into a free session and then try to force you into a contract with a whole bunch of hidden fees that you don't need.
  • Hidden Fees include Enrollment Fees, Initiation Fees, Late Fees, Processing Fees, Termination Fees Monthly Dues, First and Last Month dues paid up front! Why Bother?
  • You are locked into an open ended contract.
  • You will face overcrowded classes. You may need to share one heavy back with 1 or 2 other people.
