Westchester's Oldest
Boxing Gym

Westchester Boxing Club is Westchester's premier fitness boxing gym. We offer the perfect blend of fundamental boxing technique and the latest in full body conditioning aimed at helping you build strength and agility into your daily life. We train anyone 6 years old and up - male or female - who has a deep desire to learn the art of boxing as a means to get in shape, learn self-defense, or stop a bully in their tracks. The results you can expect are increased self-confidence, resilience and a championship mindset.

At WBC, we help you transform boxing mecanics into a fitness regimen and lifestyle.

All training sessions are personalized and private. You and your trainer are the only ones allowed in the facility durring your session. While we have always done one-on-one private training, even prior to COVID-19, for your safety and for ours, we have implemented new cleaning and scheduling protocols until further notice. The changes include:

  • A 30 minute break between sessions. This helps avoid the possibility that you may run into another member before or after your session. The break also allows us to wipe down, clean, and sanitize all exposed surfaces in the gym.
  • Temperature checks at the Front Desk before every training session.
  • We provide hand sanitzer and masks to all members.
  • Our waiting area and office are separate from each other and are away from the gym.

If you have any questions regarding these measures, feel free to call Nick at (914) 329-6287

We concentrate on what you need, testing your comfort zone, and expanding your limits. Workouts can be customized to incorporate modes in addition to boxing, such as:

  • Calisthenics
  • Bootcamp Endurance
  • Kettle Bells
  • Flexibility and Agility

Helping you transition to a new set of expectations of yourself is one way WBC helps its members live up to their potential.

Our trainers are passionate about building relationships with our clients. Trust, responsibility, and accountability is a two-way street. They are the building blocks that guide our training.

We will be with you during every punch, inspiring you, building your endurance, and helping you sculpt a fatigue resistant physique and mind.

Open 7 days a week to accomodate your busy schedule. First session starts at 5:00 am.

Unleash your inner warrior. Fight harder and dream bigger. Always remember, the biggest fight in our lives is the fight within ourselves.

Keep on punchin' !
